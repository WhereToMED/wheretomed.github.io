I'm Onboard

Finding affordable, safe and quality medication is often too time-consuming.

The cost of medication can be greatly different across pharmacies and licensed chemists in one area. These expensive prices often mean people lose out on buying the safe and quality medicine they need at affordable prices and without risking their health.

At WhereToMED we are passionate about your abiliyt to access safe, quality and affordable medicine. That is why we aim to work closely with local services to create a long lasting solution that benefit you, and your family and friends.
